Innovative footwear according your needs.

The principal functions of footwear are often sacrificed in Fashion. We have been analysing comfort elements in footwear, particularly impact absorption, shoe weight, traction and upper durability.
The methodologies used in our projects include traditional making (leather work, traditional machinery making, metal work, wood shaping) and rapid prototyping (3D milling machine, laser cutting and 3D printing).

  • Comfort.
  • Fashion Footwear.
  • Innovation and technology.
  • Made-to-measure.

Our shoes has been exhibited in London, Paris Fashion Week, New York Fashion Week, St. Petersburg, Moscow, Dutch Design Week and Barcelona 080 Fashion Week.

Our Mission

We focus on I+D research to always bring innovation and new ideas into footwear; because we believe fashion is alive and we need contemporary ideas to improve our products and make them enjoyable for our clients.

Our Plan

Every year we spend all our effort and resources in seeking new ideas and collaborating with the best professionals in engineering, to assure the best results and quality.

Our Vision

Our goal is to improve the world of fashion, specially in footwear, doing it more wearable and sustainable, without losing the aesthetics which is a key factor. The future of fashion is progress and recycling to work for a better world.
